About Nan Ellin

Nan Ellin is Dean Emeritus and Professor of Urban Design at the University of Colorado Denver's College of Architecture and Planning. She holds a Ph.D. in Urban Design from Columbia University and a B.A. in Anthropology and Hispanic Studies from Bryn Mawr College.

Her research and practice center on how cities can be designed to foster human flourishing—drawing together urban theory, ecology, public health, and social equity. She co-directs the Design/HEALth initiative and serves on the Denver Design Advisory Board.

As Dean she guided CU Denver's College of Architecture and Planning through a transformative period, championing interdisciplinary research and design's role in shaping equitable communities across Colorado and the American West.
